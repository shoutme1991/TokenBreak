About TokenBreak

Watch short-form videos while your AI works, then pause the moment it needs you.

What is TokenBreak?

TokenBreak plays Shorts, Reels, and TikTok while tools like Codex or Claude Code work in the background, then pauses the video when your AI needs input.

Why it exists

A lot of AI coding time is just waiting. TokenBreak turns that dead time into something lighter, more fun, and easier to come back from when your tool needs attention.
